Identity Management

Authentication allows a user or an application to prove that they are who they claim to be by providing valid credentials. The most common form of validation is password which is often combined with other methods like fingerprints.


With AuthWiki, a user can log into your applications with social media details, Github account, and a broad range of other credentials.
